About Me

I am a mechatronics engineering student at Tecnológico de Monterrey. I am passionate about robotics and technology, but I also enjoy manual work, such as carpentry. I am the project director for my campus's robotics team and lead the development of a service robot for the RoboCup @Home competition. What I like most about being part of robotics teams since high school is the community that forms and everything you can learn and teach from others.
